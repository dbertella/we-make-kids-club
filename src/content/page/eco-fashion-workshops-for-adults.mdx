These relaxed evening workshops are an invitation to slow down, make by hand, and reconnect with the clothes you already own.

Bring a garment you’d like to **repair**, **transform**, or **personalise**. Aurelie will provide the tools, materials, and guidance — from patching and visible mending to fabric painting and simple sewing. **Sessions are open-ended and supportive**, allowing you to explore at your own pace, whether you’re a complete beginner or have some experience.

Workshops are designed to be small, friendly, and pressure-free. They’re not project-led classes, but shared creative spaces where you decide what to make, learn through doing, and leave with something uniquely yours.

📍 Hosted in local school or community spaces

🧵 No sewing experience required

🌿 Sustainability-first, creativity-led

**What to expect**

* Bring a garment to upcycle
* Guided, freestyle making
* Sewing, painting, patching
* All tools and materials provided

**Who it’s for**

* Beginners welcome
* No sewing experience needed
* Parents, carers, creatives, curious adults
