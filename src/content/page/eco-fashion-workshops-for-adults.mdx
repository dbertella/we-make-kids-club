Adults also need a relaxed, creative space where they can reconnect, mend their clothes and rethink overconsumption.

* Maybe your child wants to make clothes but you don't know how to help them?
* Maybe you feel guilty about all those clothes sitting in your wardrobe untouched for too long?
* Maybe you want to learn how to repair your favourite clothes?
* Maybe you just want to have a bit of creative fun?
* Maybe you just want to learn a new skill and meet new people?
* Maybe you're keen to share your knowledge and meet like-minded people?

If any of the above reason rings a bell, keep on reading.

#### Current event: (Slow) Stitch & Bitch

A free monthly upcycling circle held on the second Tuesday of each month in Leytonstone. Bring a garment that needs a little love and join us for an evening of stitching, conversation and sustainable action.

Find out more about [(Slow) Stitch & Bitch](https://wemakekidsclub.com/news/Slow-Stitch--Bitch).
