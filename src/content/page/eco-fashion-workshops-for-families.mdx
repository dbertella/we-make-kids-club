### Creative experiences for children who love to make, explore and express themselves

I offer hands-on eco-fashion experiences designed to spark creativity, build confidence, and introduce children to a more thoughtful way of engaging with fashion.

Whether it’s a birthday celebration, a pop-up event, or a weekly club, each session is designed to feel special, relaxed, and inspiring.

### Birthday Parties

##### A creative, stylish & sustainable celebration (ages 5–10)

A birthday party that goes beyond entertainment: children design and create something they’re proud to wear and take home.

Perfect for small groups and meaningful celebrations.

What’s included:

* 90-minute guided creative workshop
* Up to 8 children
* All materials provided (fabrics, paints, haberdashery, tools)
* A personalised, hands-on experience
* Each child leaves with their own wearable creation

What children will do:
Children explore eco-fashion by designing and decorating their own piece. They get to choose their technique: fabric paint, sewing or fabric glue, embroidery, drawing and lots of accessories from the haberdashery bar (buttons, ribbons, pom pom trim, etc.)

### Eco-Fashion Club

For children who want to go further, I run small group clubs where we explore design, making, and sustainability over a series of sessions.

These sessions allow children to:

* Develop their own ideas over time
* Learn new techniques
* Build confidence in their creative voice

👉 See the [For Schools](https://wemakekidsclub.com/eco-fashion-workshops-for-schools) page for more details on how these sessions work.

#### Why eco-fashion?

Children are naturally creative and curious.
Through making and experimenting, they begin to understand that clothing doesn’t have to be disposable—it can be transformed, personalised, and valued.

The aim isn’t just to make something, but to see things differently.
